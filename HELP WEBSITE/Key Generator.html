Help | Key Generator

Thank you for choosing Key Generator to generate your passwords and serial keys! Below, you will find topics aimed to assist you in properly using the program.

‍

Registering

Before using the program, you need to register yourself. Below are the steps required to properly register yourself:

  1. Enter your email address in the email address field.
  2. Enter your password in the password field.
  3. Solve the CAPTCHA and enter your solution in the captcha field (you can click on the refresh button to get a new captcha).
  4. Click on the Register button.
Note: You must save your PIN Code in a safe place, as you require it for every login, and without it, you will not be able to access the program.

‍

Signing In

Once you have registered yourself, you are able to sign in and use the program. Below are the steps required to sign in to your account:

  1. Enter your email address in the email address field (the one you used when registering).
  2. Enter your password in the password field (the one you used when registering).
  3. Solve the CAPTCHA and enter your solution in the captcha field (you can click on the refresh button to get a new captcha).
  4. Click on the Sign In button.
Note: Now you have to enter the PIN Code that you received when registering. You have to enter it on every sign in (for your security).

‍

Generating a Password

Once you have signed in, you are able to choose from generating a password, and generating a serial key. Below are the instructions for a password:

  1. Select the password type from the check boxes on the left side (you can choose multiple types, but you must at least choose one).
  2. Enter the length of the password in the corresponding box (it must at least be 5).
  3. Click on the Generate button.
Your password can be found in the text field next to the Generate button.

‍

Generating a Serial Key(s)

Once you have signed in, you are able to choose from generating a password, and generating a serial key. Below are the instructions for serial keys:

  1. Select the type of serial key from the check boxes on the left side.
  2. Enter the length of the key in the corresponding box.
  3. Click on the Generate button.

You can optionally include a delimiter in your serial keys:

  1. Check the 'Add Delimiter' field.
  2. Enter your desired delimiter in the Delimiter field.
  3. Specify a delimiter location in the field (where the delimiter is in your key).

Without Delimiter: ABC3DEF4RS3T

With Delimiter: ABC3-DEF4-RS3T (settings: Delimiter -, Delimiter Location 5)

Your serial keys can be found in the text box next to 'Serial Keys'.
